## FAQ

**Q: Does the app upload my videos?**
No. Pose detection runs entirely on-device using Core ML. Only anonymized pose landmark data is sent to our server for form scoring. Your videos never leave your phone.

**Q: What camera angle works best?**
Position your camera at a slight angle (about 45 degrees) where your full body is visible throughout the exercise. Avoid direct front or back angles.

**Q: Why is my score low?**
The scoring system evaluates multiple technique categories per exercise. Tap on your workout in the Library to see the detailed breakdown and coaching tips for improvement.
