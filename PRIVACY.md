# Privacy Policy for Kinetic Eye

**Last Updated: April 3, 2026**

## Overview

Kinetic Eye ("the App") is an exercise form analysis app that uses computer vision to score your workout technique. Your privacy is important to us.

## Data Collection

### What We Collect
- **Pose landmark data**: Anonymized body position coordinates (33 skeletal points per frame) are sent to our server for form scoring. This data contains no personally identifiable information — it is purely numerical joint coordinates.

### What We Do NOT Collect
- **Videos**: Your exercise videos are never uploaded. All video processing and pose detection happens entirely on your device using Core ML.
- **Personal information**: We do not collect names, email addresses, accounts, or any personal identifiers.
- **Analytics or tracking**: We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.
- **Location data**: We do not access or collect your location.

## Data Storage

- All workout sessions, videos, scores, and profile data are stored locally on your device.
- Pose landmark data sent to our server for scoring is processed in real time and is not stored after the response is returned.

## Third-Party Services

- The App uses a server hosted on Railway for form scoring analysis. Only anonymized pose landmark coordinates are transmitted. No video, image, or personal data is sent.
- No third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services are used.

## Camera and Photo Library

- **Camera**: Used solely to record exercise videos for analysis. Videos are stored locally on your device.
- **Photo Library**: Used to select existing videos for analysis and to save exported videos with skeleton overlays.

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any data from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted to this page with an updated date.

## Contact
